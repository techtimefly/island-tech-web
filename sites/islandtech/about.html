About Island Tech IO

Security consulting grounded in engineering reality.

I founded Island Tech IO to help teams make security tools, developer workflows, and remediation guidance useful in real engineering environments.

Founder

Tremaine Island

Former software engineer turned application security engineer.

Origin and service

I am originally from Dublin, Georgia, and I served honorably in the Army Reserve from 2002 to 2010, including a 2005-2006 deployment to Iraq.

Education and early career

I earned my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Macon State College, now part of Middle Georgia State University, while working as a software engineer contractor at Robins Air Force Base.

Developer-to-AppSec perspective

My path moved from Army Reserve service, to college IT support, to software engineering, and then into application security engineering. That gives me a practical view of how code is written, how teams ship, and how findings need to be validated, explained, prioritized, and fixed.

Tooling and DevSecOps focus

I have integrated both open source and commercial AppSec tools into CI/CD pipelines so developers can receive security feedback earlier and with less friction. My experience includes false positive analysis, remediation guidance, secure development workflows, and improving security-tool signal quality.

Denver and hands-on practice

I have lived in the Denver metro area since 2015. Outside of consulting work, I build and maintain a Proxmox-based homelab, home network, and self-hosted applications, which keeps my technical practice operational and hands-on.
