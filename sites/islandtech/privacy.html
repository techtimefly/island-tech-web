Privacy Policy

Plain-language privacy for website inquiries.

This page explains what information Island Tech IO collects through this website and how it is used.

July 10, 2026 Effective date

Information collected

If you submit the contact form or email Island Tech IO, I may collect your name, email address, organization, service interest, message content, and basic delivery metadata needed to receive and respond to the inquiry.

How information is used

I use inquiry information to respond to messages, understand the type of help requested, provide consulting follow-up, maintain business records, and protect the site from spam or abuse.

Sensitive information

Please do not submit passwords, secrets, source code, regulated data, confidential vulnerability details, protected health information, or other sensitive material through the public contact form. If sensitive security details are needed, I will coordinate an appropriate follow-up process.

Third-party processors

This site may use Web3Forms for form handling, Proton Mail and SimpleLogin for email routing, GitHub Pages or another hosting provider for static hosting, and homelab infrastructure for internal preview deployments.

Retention

I keep inquiry records as long as needed to respond, manage business follow-up, maintain records, or meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of inquiry information by emailing contact@islandtech.io.

Analytics and cookies

This site does not currently use advertising cookies or analytics scripts. If analytics are added later, this policy should be updated to describe what is collected and why.

No sale of personal information

I do not sell personal information submitted through this website.

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email contact@islandtech.io.
